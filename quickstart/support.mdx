## Core Team Contact
<Warning>Direct contact to the Compass Core Team should only be made if a critical bug or vulnerability has been identified.</Warning>
If you find a bug or vulnerability within Compass or have a technical question regarding the bot, you may want the Compass Core Team either via ticket support or via Direct Messages.

## Community Support
Please do consider asking the community or our dedicated Support Team in the Compass Support server. Tickets and emails can pile up on our end, so responses may be delayed.
